Super energy boosting smoothies are a way to feel energized for the whole day.

Eating healthy diet packed with vitamins and minerals is good way how to feel energized for the whole day and have the necessary energy to complete daily tasks, be succesful at work or at school. Some activities are draining, especilly when we are under huge stress or when some tasks are too demanding to complete, we need that boost. But where to get it? Our bodies need some fuel from the outside world, they are not able to create energy from nothing or even worse - from fast food or processed food. Think of it as when you have a plant or a car.. When you take care of it patiently and you give it, what it needs, it will give you something back, some reward.

Fruit and vegetables full of vitamins.

We're not talking here about protein and healthy fats from meat and fish or even from eggs. For the refreshing smoothies are not suitable. Also lots of people have in mind, the more sweet the food is, the more energy they will get, right? It is not always true.

Sugary drinks, candy, and pastries put too much fuel (sugar) into our blood too quickly. The ensuing crash leaves us tired and hungry again after short amount of time. But with the healthy fats, complex carbohydrates and protein, body takes longer to digest. So it will satisfy your hunger, and provide a slow, steady stream of energy, which is exactly what we are looking for.

Few examples of good, energizing fruit and veggies are:

  1. Coconuts, avocados, nuts and seeds - for their high healthy fat content.
  2. Bananas, apples - for the quick energy.
  3. Pineapples, oranges, mangos, lemons, grapefruits - for their high vitamin C content.
  4. Spinach and kale - as a good source of nutriets providing energy.

1.Pineapple Kale Energy Smoothie

Ingredients for this energy smoothie are:

  • 1/4 pineapple, fresh or frozen
  • 1 cup of kale leaves
  • 1 tbsp of coconut oil
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1 cup of water/coconut water
  • optional! matcha green tea powder

Add the pineapple, kale and avocado to the blender. Pour the water or coconut water inside and add 1 tablespoon of coconut oil. You can also add 1 teaspoon of matcha green tea powder for extra energy boost and flavour. Blend it all together and enjoy.

2.Sunburst Energy Smoothie

To make this smoothie full of vitamin C you will need:

  • 1/2 pineapple, frozen or fresh
  • 1/2 mango, frozen or fresh
  • 3/4 cup 100% orange juice
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds

Put fruit and chia seeds inside the blender and mix it together, until the texture is really smooth. Then pour over the mixture 3/4 cup of fresh orange juice and blend it again until it is mixed well together.You can sprinkle some more chia seeds over the top. Enjoy!
