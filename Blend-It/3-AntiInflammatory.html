1.Carrot Ginger Turmeric Smoothie

To make this delicious, fruity smoothie you will need:

  • 2 carrots
  • 1 large ripe banana
  • 1/3 fresh pineapple
  • 1/2 Tbsp fresh ginger
  • 1/4 tsp ground turmeric (or cinnamon)
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk

The whole process is quite easy. Just peel the carrots, ginger and lemon. Cut everything to smaller pieces and put them in the blender. Add 1/4 cup of water and 1 cup of almond milk inside. Blend and enjoy.

2.Cherry-Spinach Smoothie

To make this smoothie full of antioxidants you will need:

  • 1 cup plain low-fat kefir
  • 1 cup frozen/fresh cherries
  • 1/2 cup baby spinach leaves
  • 1/4 fresh ripe avocado
  • 1 tbs of salted almond butter
  • 1 small peeled ginger
  • 1 tsp chia seeds

Firstly, you will need to put the kefir inside the blender. Then add remaining ingredients including chia seeds and peeled ginger. Mix it well until the texture is smooth. You can also add water if the texture is too thick. On the top you can add some more chia seeds. Enjoy!
