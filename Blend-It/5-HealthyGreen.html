Green smoothies are delicious too!

Green smoothies are uniquie for it's green colour and they also differ from others for their ingredients. Most green smoothies are made from leafy greens, which are packed with antioxidants, vitamin C, calcium and magnesium. So if someone wants to add more vegetables into their diet, this could be very good option. Green smoothies are not only made of greens, their content is usually 60% of fruit and 40% of vegetables, which makes it delicious even for people who are not fans of vegetables. It's mild sweetness is popular even among children and younger generation.

Reasons to go for green smoothies:

  1. Very nutritious, full of fibre.
  2. Easy to digest.
  3. Good habit of eating more greens.
  4. Healthier than usual vegetable and fruit juices.
  5. Low in calories, but really filling.

1.Matcha Pear Green Protein Smoothie

What do you need for this protein packed smoothie:

  • 1 scoop of vanilla protein shake
  • 1 cup of unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 cup of spinach
  • 1 pear
  • 1/2 tsp of matcha tea powder

Put protein shake and almond milk into the blender. Mix it with spoon to combine. Then put spinach, sliced pear and matcha tea powder to the mixture. Blend it altogether until smooth. You can also add some extra water or more almond milk if it's too thick. Enjoy.

2.Spring Detox Smoothie

You will need these ingredients:

  • 1 cup of green tea (chilled)
  • 1 cup of cilantro
  • 1 cup of baby kale
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 1/3 pineapple
  • 1 lemon (squeezed)
  • 1 tbsp of greshly grated ginger
  • 1/2 avocado

Instructions are quite easy, just pour the green tea inside the blender. Add chopped cilantro, baby kale, chopped cucumber and pineapple. Then grate the ginger and squeeze the lemon over the ingredients. At the end add the avocado and start blending. Enjoy!
